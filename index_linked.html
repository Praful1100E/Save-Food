How FoodShare Works

Simple steps to make a big impact

1. Post Your Surplus

Restaurants, grocery stores, and households can easily post available food items with details like quantity, expiry date, and pickup location.

2. Find & Request

NGOs, shelters, and individuals can browse available food, filter by location and preferences, and send pickup requests.

3. Pickup & Share

Coordinate pickup times and locations. Track your impact and help build a community around reducing food waste.

About FoodShare

Learn more about our mission and the team behind it

Our Mission

FoodShare is dedicated to reducing food waste while fighting hunger in communities. We believe that no food should go to waste when there are people in need.

Meet the Founders

Praful Thakur

Founder and lead developer of FoodShare. Passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems and create positive social impact.

Jatin Dadwal

Co-founder and business strategist. Dedicated to building sustainable solutions that benefit both the environment and local communities.

Our Story

FoodShare was born from a simple idea: what if we could connect those who have surplus food with those who need it? Praful and Jatin worked together to transform this vision into reality, combining their technical expertise and passion for social good.

Through countless hours of collaboration, research, and development, they created a platform that not only reduces food waste but also strengthens community bonds and helps fight hunger in meaningful ways.
